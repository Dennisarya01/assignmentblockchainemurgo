Business Process

1.Customers come to the cinema.
2.Cinema Shows existing Movies.
3.The customer chooses the film he wants to watch in the cinema.
4.The customer sees the available seats.
5.The customer makes a seat reservation.
6.Customers make payments.
7.If the customer's money is in excess, the difference will be refunded.
8.The customer has successfully purchased a ticket.
9.The customer prints a ticket to enter the cinema.
